How It Works
STEP 01
Sequential Analysis
Files are analyzed one at a time to stay within memory limits. Each HAR is fully scanned then discarded before the next begins.
STEP 02
Risk Scoring
Each file is scored 0–100 based on plaintext PII violations, hashed data transmissions, vendors receiving data, and estimated damages.
STEP 03
Ranked Results
Files are ranked worst-to-best. Drill into any file for the full single-file analysis view with request details and legal context.
Risk Grade Reference
CRITICAL
Any plaintext PII to third parties
Any plaintext personal data (email, phone, name) intercepted by a third-party vendor. Strongest litigation candidates under CIPA §631.
HIGH RISK
Hashed PII or multiple vendors
Hashed PII transmissions enabling cross-site tracking, or two or more vendors receiving personal data without clear consent.
ELEVATED
Limited tracking exposure
Third-party tracking present but no confirmed PII transmission detected. Consent mechanism may be absent.
LOWER RISK
Minimal or no violations
No PII violations detected. Standard anonymous analytics only.
